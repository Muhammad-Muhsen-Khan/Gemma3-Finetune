Peripheral Angiography

Puncture Site Care

For one week:
_x0095_Do not take a tub bath, go swimming or use a Jacuzzi or hot 
tub.
_x0095_Use only mild soap and water to gently clean the area around 
the puncture site. 
_x0095_Gently pat the puncture site dry after showering. 
_x0095_Do not use powders, lotions, or ointments in the area of the 
puncture site. 

You may remove the bandage and shower the day after the 
procedure.  You may leave the bandage off. 

You may have a small bruise around the puncture site.  This is 
normal and will go away one-two weeks. 

Activity 

For the first 48 hours: 
1.Do not drive for 48 hours after the procedure

For the first week:
_x0095_Do not lift, push , pull or carry anything heavier than 10 
pounds
_x0095_Do not do any exercises or activity that causes you to hold 
your breath or bear down with abdominal muscles.   Take care not 
to put strain on your abdominal muscles when coughing, sneezing, 
or moving your bowels.

After one week:
_x0095_You may go back to all your regular activities, including 
sexual activity.  We suggest you begin your exercise program at 
half of your usual routine for the first few days.   You may 
then gradually work back to your full routine.
